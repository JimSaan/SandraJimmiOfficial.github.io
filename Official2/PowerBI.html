POWER BI DASHBOARD

Data Professional Survey Breakdown Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard offering a comprehensive analysis of a survey conducted among data professionals. This dashboard provides detailed insights into several key aspects of the respondents' demographics, job titles, salaries, and overall job satisfaction.

Key Features:
Salary by Country: Visualizes the average salary distribution among data professionals across different countries, including the United States, India, the United Kingdom, Canada, and other regions.
Average Salary by Job Title: Compares the average salaries across various data-related job titles such as Data Scientist, Data Engineer, Data Architect, Data Analyst, and more, helping users identify income trends in different roles.
Favorite Programming Languages: Analyzes the most preferred programming languages among data professionals, categorized by job titles, revealing trends in language preference like Python, R, C/C++, and others.
Average Salary by Gender: Displays the gender-based salary differences among survey participants, enabling users to explore insights into gender wage gaps in the field.
Happiness X Work-Life Balance & Happiness X Salary: Two key gauges show the average scores for job satisfaction and the relationship between salary and work-life balance, offering a quick overview of well-being in the profession.
Participant Demographics: Provides an overview of the survey, including the total number of participants (630), and the average age of respondents (29.87 years).

This Power BI dashboard offers various aspects of the data profession landscape in detail. It is a valuable tool for HR professionals, industry researchers, and data professionals to navigate salary trends, job satisfaction, and programming language preferences in the data field.
