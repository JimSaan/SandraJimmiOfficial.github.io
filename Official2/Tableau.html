Netflix Movies and TV Shows Analysis Dashboard

An interactive Tableau dashboard offering an extensive analysis of Netflix's movies and TV shows. Gain insights into the distribution of total movies and TV shows across various countries, coupled with the top 10 genres and ratings.

This dashboard presents a detailed profile of a selected movie or TV show, including the description, duration, rating, release year, and date. It is fully interactive, allowing users to tailor their exploration between movies and TV shows.

With an intuitive selection feature for the type of show (movies, TV shows), and the corresponding name of the movie or TV show, this dashboard provides an in-depth, personalized analysis of Netflix's extensive catalogue. A valuable tool for media analysts, content strategists, and Netflix enthusiasts to navigate and understand the rich landscape of Netflix's offerings.

Financial Complaints and Resolution Analysis Dashboard

This comprehensive dashboard provides a deep dive into the analysis of financial complaints and their resolutions. It displays key metrics such as total complaints received, timely response rate, complaints in progress, and complaints resolved at no cost, delivering a quantitative understanding of the firm's complaint management efficiency.

Explore a range of graphical representations including a detailed graph showcasing the variety and volume of complaints types, a geospatial hexagonal map illustrating the distribution of complaints across the US states, and an analysis of complaints by product type. It also offers insights into complaint channels, showing the media through which the complaints were received.

Furthermore, a donut chart presents the number of customers who disputed, bringing the overall customer satisfaction picture into focus. This interactive and informative dashboard is an invaluable tool for financial analysts, customer service managers, and stakeholders to strategize efficient complaint handling and improve customer satisfaction.

SQL-Based COVID-19 Data Exploration and Visualization

A compelling visualization of SQL exploration on global COVID-19 datasets from 2020 to 2023. This data presentation paints a comprehensive picture of the pandemic's impact, detailing total COVID-19 cases, deaths, and the corresponding death percentage globally.

Explore a detailed graph portraying the distribution of deaths across continents and a color-intensity world map illustrating the percentage of population affected per country. Concluding with a line graph that chronicles the progression of the infection in the top 6 most affected countries, the visualization offers a multifaceted perspective of the pandemic's global impact.

This project is not just a visualization but also a testament to the powerful combination of SQL and data science, providing a comprehensive tool for health analysts, policy makers, and the general public to understand and navigate the global landscape of the COVID-19 pandemic.

Global Alcohol Consumption Analysis

A simple visualization exploring the distribution of alcohol consumption across the globe. This project provides an engaging overview of per capita alcohol consumption, represented both geographically through a world map and quantitatively via a bar graph.

By depicting the liters of alcohol consumed per capita, this visualization provides a clear picture of the global drinking habits. It serves as a practical resource for health analysts, policy makers, or anyone interested in understanding global alcohol consumption patterns.
