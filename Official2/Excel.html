Comprehensive Sales Analysis Dashboard (2015-2018)

This comprehensive Excel-based Sales Analysis Dashboard provides a holistic view of product sales from 2015 to 2018. It presents detailed insights into the performance of different sales personnel, sales channels, product types, and regions, helping to identify key trends and areas for improvement.

The dashboard utilizes Pivot Tables and Graphs to showcase the cumulative sales for various products, stratified by salesperson and region. It features three distinct product types and three different sales channels, highlighting the versatility of the business model and the diversity of its offerings.

Users can dynamically interact with the data through intuitive slicers for salespersons, regions, product types, and sales channels. This facilitates more in-depth, bespoke analysis and allows users to focus on specific aspects of the sales data at a granular level.

In essence, this Sales Analysis Dashboard is a potent tool for business analysts, sales managers, and stakeholders to drive data-driven decision-making and strategy formulation.
